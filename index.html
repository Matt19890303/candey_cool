ABOUT US

Candey Cool is run by Neil Candey, a Qualified Automotive Diagnostic Technician with over 18 years experience in Auto Aircon services, Electrical & Diagnostics.

We take pride in our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, and we strive to provide our customers with the best possible experience with us. So whether you're looking to do maintenance on your vehicle's air conditioning system or need repairs for an existing one, we're here to help.

SERVICES

Candey Cool provides Commercial automotive aircon services for Cars, Trucks, Reach Stackers & Earthmoving Vehicles. We are registered for all GOVERNMENT-OWNED vehicles. As a Durban based mobile service we specialise in VW and Audi and provide car diagnostic testing.

Aircon repair

If you're experiencing problems with your air conditioner, we can diagnose and repair any issues quickly and effectively. Our experienced technician can work on all makes and models, and we use the latest tools and techniques to get your air conditioner working like new.

Aircon service

Regular maintenance is necessary to keep your air conditioner running smoothly and to prevent breakdowns. Our maintenance services include checks of refrigerant levels, cleaning of air filters, and inspections of hoses and fittings.

Air duct cleaning

Over time, your air ducts can become clogged with dust and debris, which can affect the performance of your air conditioner. Our air duct cleaning services can help improve air flow and efficiency, and promote better air quality in your vehicle.

Electrical diagnostics

If your air conditioner is not blowing cold air, it may be low on refrigerant. Our coolant refill services can quickly and safely add refrigerant to your air conditioner, so you can enjoy cool air again.
